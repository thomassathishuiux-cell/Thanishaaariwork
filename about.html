Why we are different

100% Unique Design

At Nangaya Designs, our creations are as unique as yours. We believe in the magic of individuality and let it inspire our work. Each design is a testament to our commitment to originality. Our skilled artisans, led by Preethi Ezhilan, pour their creativity and mastery into every piece. We don’t just follow trends; we set them. With us, you’ll find pieces that are not just Aari work but also unique art forms that carry their own distinct stories, mirroring our team’s rich tradition and innovative spirit.

Guaranteed Customer Satisfaction

Your satisfaction is our topmost priority. We pride ourselves on maintaining an uncompromised quality standard and delivering products that exceed your expectations. From the moment you choose us to when you receive your Aari work, we ensure a seamless, delightful experience. Our dedicated customer support is always ready to assist you with your queries and concerns. At Nangaya Designs, we don’t just aim to meet your expectations; we strive to surpass them.

High Customization

We understand that everyone has unique tastes and preferences. This is why we offer a high level of customization for our clients. You have the freedom to choose your designs, colours, patterns, and sizes, ensuring the final product is exactly what you envisioned. Our team works closely with you to understand your preferences and translates them into the art of Aari work. At Nangaya Designs, we’re not just creating Aari designs but tailoring dreams into reality.

Weaving Wonders in Every Stitch

Why choose US?

Over the years, I’ve been privileged to master the techniques and beauty of Aari embroidery. It has become more than just a passion; it’s an important part of my identity.

My team and I, all skilled artisans with more than 20 years of experience, work tirelessly to bring the magic of Aari work to life. Each of us at Nangaya Designs is committed to maintaining the rich heritage of this ancient craft.

At Nangaya Designs, we don’t just create products; we tell stories through our stitches, and craft legacies and pay tribute to the art of Aari work. Each piece is a testament to our unwavering dedication, creativity, and commitment to quality.
